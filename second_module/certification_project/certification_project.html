Geralt of Rivia

Butcher of Blaviken

Here’s a timeline of Geralt of Rivia based on the books and games:

Early Years (Pre-1250s)

  • Born around 1174 in Kaer Morhen, the stronghold of the Witchers of the Wolf School.
  • His mother, Visenna, a sorceress, abandons him at Kaer Morhen.
  • Undergoes the Trial of the Grasses, a brutal mutation process that grants him enhanced abilities.
  • Survives additional experimental mutations, making him faster and stronger than other witchers.
  • Trained by Vesemir, his mentor and father figure.
  • Given the nickname “White Wolf” due to his pale hair and wolf medallion.
  • Leaves Kaer Morhen to begin his life as a witcher.

The Early Adventures (1250s - 1260s)

  • Travels across the Northern Kingdoms, hunting monsters for coin.
  • Encounters Yennefer of Vengerberg, a powerful sorceress. Their tumultuous romance begins after a djinn binds their fates.
  • Becomes the Butcher of Blaviken after killing Renfri and her gang in self-defense.
  • Gains a “Child of Surprise,” Ciri, after invoking the Law of Surprise when saving Duny (Ciri’s father).

The Saga Begins (1260s - 1270s)

  • Meets Jaskier (Dandelion), a bard who becomes his closest friend.
  • Spends years searching for Ciri, unaware of her destiny.
  • Finally reunites with Ciri in Brokilon Forest, but lets her go.
  • Later, finds her in Cintra, but is imprisoned when he tries to claim her.
  • Cintra falls to the Nilfgaardian Empire. Queen Calanthe dies, and Ciri disappears.
  • Geralt desperately searches for Ciri, believing she survived.

The Witcher Saga (1270s - 1280s)

  • Reunites with Yennefer and Ciri, training the girl in Kaer Morhen.
  • Protects Ciri from Nilfgaard, the Wild Hunt, and other threats.
  • Takes Ciri to Aretuza, where she learns magic.
  • Ciri becomes a fugitive after the Thanedd Coup, a bloody conflict between mages.
  • Geralt, severely wounded, is rescued by dryads in Brokilon.
  • Forms “The Hansa”, a group of companions helping him find Ciri.
  • Finds Yennefer enslaved in Nilfgaard and rescues her.
  • Ciri eventually escapes to another world but later returns.
  • In Rivia, Geralt is mortally wounded by a peasant mob.
  • Yennefer tries to heal him, but they both vanish.

The Witcher Games (1274 - 1300s, Non-Canon to Books)

  • Geralt mysteriously returns from death with no memory.
  • Rescued by the Witchers of Kaer Morhen.
  • Fights the Salamandra, a criminal organization.
  • Recovers some of his past memories while dealing with political conflicts.

The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings (1274-1275)

  • Framed for the assassination of King Foltest.
  • Goes on the run to clear his name.
  • Discovers Letho of Gulet, another witcher, is behind the assassinations.
  • Regains more memories of his past, including Yennefer’s existence.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (1276-1277)

  • Fully regains his memory.
  • Searches for Ciri, now pursued by the Wild Hunt.
  • Finds Ciri and helps her face her destiny.
  • Depending on choices:
  • Ciri becomes a Witcher, an Empress, or disappears.
  • Defeats the Wild Hunt and Emperor Emhyr.

Later Years (Post-1277, Uncertain Fate)

  • Depending on choices, Geralt may:
  • Retire with Yennefer or Triss.
  • Continue as a lone witcher.
  • Die or vanish in some endings.
